TapeCalc

TapeCalc emulates a time tested model of a classic tape based calculator with all the modern conveniences expected in a modern desktop utility. Available to purchase on the Mac App Store.

Bright Display

Bright Display is a digital boxoffice system for movie theatres. This system allows you to design and control Menu Boards, Mylars, Event Times and more.

RoDesk Remote

RoDesk Remote lets you control your Roku media player from your desktop. You have all the standard Roku controls plus the Channel list for quick navigation. Available to purchase on the Mac App Store.
